#Superstore Performance Dashboard (Excel)

##Project Overview:
An interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze and visualize the sales and profit performance of a retail superstore. The goal is to transform raw data into actionable insights for better business decision-making.

##Key Features:
- Interactive Slicers: Filter data by Segment, Region, and Category.
- KPI Cards: High-level summary of Total Profit, Sales, and Orders.
- Trend Analysis: Yearly profit trends from 2014 to 2017.
- Geographical Insights: Profit share analysis across different regions.
  
##Key Insights:
- Efficiency: Achieved 95% of profit targets and 92% of sales targets.
- Top Performer: The Technology category is the main driver of profit.
- Regional Success: The West region leads in overall profitability.

##Tools Used:
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Power Query, Slicers, Dashboard Design)
